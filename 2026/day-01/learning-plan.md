# What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?
- Devops is combination of Development and operation, it is culture or mindset where development team and operation team combine to reduce time to market, automating task, and sacle to end user.
- Cloud Engineering focuses on designing, building, securing, and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure and services instead of managing physical servers.
 - In simple terms, DevOps is more about how software is delivered, while Cloud Engineering is more about where and how the infrastructure is built and run.
